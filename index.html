How to Play

Goal: Reach the Golden Treasure Chest with at least 5 treasure coins.

  1. Roll the dice and move that many spaces.
  2. Solve the math question for the space you land on.
  3. Correct answer: collect 1 coin. Treasure Chest space: collect 2 coins.
  4. Wrong answer: move back 2 spaces and collect no coin.
  5. Shark Attack wrong answer: move back 3 spaces.
  6. Storm wrong answer: lose your next turn.
  7. Pirate Trap wrong answer: lose 1 coin.
  8. If you reach the finish without 5 coins, keep answering questions until you have enough.

Modes: Local Players is pass-and-play. CPU mode makes players 2–4 automatic.
